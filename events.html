Events

Here are some events our members have participated in!

DSTA Cyber Defenders Discovery Camp 2019

A Cyber Security Capture the Flag Competition, where students solve Cyber Security Problems regarding Web explotation, Networking, Reverse Engineering, Open Source Intelligence and more!

GeekCamp

Geekcamp is a one-day event that brings together hundreds of geeks to share about technologies and knowledge through curated talks, workshops and demonstrations. Some of the more memorable projects showcased this year include an image-to-music converter and motion-controlled robotic arms.

Tech For Good

Tech For Good is an innovation challenge for passionate and creative youths to develop innovative solutions that will benefit people with disabilities. Throughout the challenge, our members worked with Tech For Good mentors to develop attachable safety lights for the visually impaired, showcasing their finished prototypes at the Tech For Good festival held at NLB!
